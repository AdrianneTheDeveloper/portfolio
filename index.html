About Me

I started my coding journey as a student studying English. The path I was on couldn't be further away from the huge world of tech. My interest is technology began to pique when I wanted to game on my Chromebook. I was introduced to the Linux Dev Environment and fell in love.

Since then my passion in building, maintaining, and designing amazing applications has only grown. I am also an artist by hobby. These two traits fuel by drive to have apps jam-packed with creativity and show my passion. I also strive to create things that are highly accessible to all. I am eager to work with a group of programmers who hold those same values!
